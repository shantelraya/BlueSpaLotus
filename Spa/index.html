About Us

Our guests come for the experience of knowing we take great care and dedicate the time necessary for our nurturing treatments. Nasya

Abhyanga

The widely popular and traditional self care go-to in Ayurveda is Abhyanga. Warm, medicinal, Ayurvedic oils are used for a soothing, grounding and nurturing rhythmic massage.

Shirodhara

Is the application of warmed oil to the forehead (3rd eye) which then soothingly coats the entire scalp. Shirodhara is performed with the client reclined in a relaxing environment and is traditionally used to calm the nerves and purify the mind.

Choorna Swedana

Involves muslin cloth bags filled with grains and herbs dipped in warm herbal oil and massaged onto the body in a compression and rolling type motion to release muscle tension.
